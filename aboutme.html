About me

My Story

My name is Franziska v. der Ropp-Cramm, I am 28 years old and I am passionate aboutUI/UX Design.

My professional career started with a studies in Game Art and 3D Animation at the SAE Institute in Stuttgart,which I successfully completed until September 2019.

After my bachelor studies which I finished with the highest grade (1st class), I was lucky enough to apply my acquired knowledge and skills as a 3D Artist at AR-Werk GmbH. In this position I was able to expand my knowledge in the 3D field and at the same time I developed a strong interest in web design and application design. Since AR-Werk GmbH was a startup, I also took on cross-positional tasks that allowed me to take my first professional steps in the field of UI/UX design.

Unfortunately, my employment ended during the Corona pandemic, a time that provided many challenges, especially in finding a suitable position as a 3D artist. During this time, however, a strong desire developed in me to delve more deeply into UI/UX design and acquire further skills in this area. For this reason, I participated in various UI/UX online courses on the platform Udemy, started a voluntary project on wix.com and decided to take the full-time course "UX Design Program" from Careerfoundry. During this highly practical course, I gained extensive insight into the varied tasks and activities of a UX designer and had the opportunity to put user-centered designs into practice. The highlight of my work during this course was undoubtedly the development of my own interactive prototype - Olive, the health app.

Being employed by Seeburger AG made me realize how important it is, as a designer, to follow the processes and procedures of the developers to ensure a smooth workflow in order to create innovative and user-centered solutions. For this reason, I decided to deal more intensively with frontend development and to gain additional competencies in this area through further education.

Currently, I am therefore now attending the full-time online course "Frontend Development for Designers" via the platform CareerFoundry, which started on 11.09. and has a duration of 2 months. In this course, which is also very practice-oriented, I am learning how to use HTML, CSS and JavaScript on my own in order to further deepen my skills as a UI/UX designer.
