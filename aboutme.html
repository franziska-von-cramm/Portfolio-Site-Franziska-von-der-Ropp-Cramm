About Me

My name is Franziska v. der Ropp-Cramm and I am 28 years old. After my bachelor studies, which I finished with the best grade in 2019, I had the opportunity to apply my acquired knowledge and skills as a 3D artist at AR-Werk GmbH. Since AR-Werk GmbH was a startup, I also took on cross-job tasks that gave me my first insights and professional steps in the field of UI/UX design. In this way, I was able to expand my expertise in 3D while developing a strong interest in web and application design.

Motivation

My professional goal changed when I was employed by AR-Werk because I had insights into the field of UI/UX design. I have discovered a new field of interest, which gave me a lot of pleasure in its implementation. A desire has arisen in me to further explore UI/UX design and gain more knowledge in this field.

UX Design Program - Career Foundry

During this highly hands-on course, I gained extensive insight into the varied tasks and activities of a UX designer and had the opportunity to put user-centered designs into practice. The highlight of my work during this course was undoubtedly the development of my own interactive prototype - Olive, the health app.

Being employed at Seeburger AG as a UI Designer/ Visual Designer made me realize how important it is as a designer to be able to follow the processes and workflows of the developers to ensure a smooth workflow and thus create innovative and user-centered solutions. Therefore, I decided to deal more intensively with frontend development and to gain additional competencies in this area through further education.

In the context of this advanced training I program my portfolio site from scratch with HTML, CSS and Java Script with all important functions and content. I will continue to maintain it after the completion of the training and take up new projects.
